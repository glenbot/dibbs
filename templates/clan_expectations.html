Clan Expectations

We want members to enjoy their time in clan chat as well as while playing the game. We expect members to respect one another. This means no cursing, or personal attacks on members.

Donate only what is requested. When requesting, do not ask for levels so that all members can participate. (Exception made for war, where level 4 drags, 5/6 wizards 5/6 archers are used.)

Donations are filled quickly in our clan. Leave your chat open for a better chance to donate.

We like to help, especially new members and members with low level THs. We expect all members to at least keep a 1:2 ratio for donations and requests. For example, at least 100 donations for 200 requests, or 600 donations and 1200 requests. You can become an Elder by donating 500 troops in a season. We expect elders to at least donate 200 per season to maintain elder.

We use the app Groupme for clan chat outside of Clash of Clans. It is available on Android and IOS. Register with your Clash of Clans name, and an email address. Provide this info to a leader in Clash of Clans chat, and we will get you added to our chat group.
